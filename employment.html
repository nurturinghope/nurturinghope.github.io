Responsibilities:

Psychiatric Rehabilitation Program (PRP) Coordinators

Program:

  • Adult and Children Program

Location:

  • Baltimore County and Baltimore City

Employment Status:

  • Part-time Contractual

Responsibilities:

  • Lead groups and provide services individually in areas that are in alignment
    with client’s treatment plan.
  • Assist and develop activities for both groups and individuals to enhance functioning
    consistent with the member’s Individual Rehabilitation Plan (IRP).
  • Close coordination with the Rehab Specialist.
  • Monitor and increase quality of PRP services to members even if that requires actions
    outside the job description as long as the supervisor is informed.
  • Report orally weekly to supervisor on performance of groups and individuals.
  • Coordinate with client about their goals.
  • Show evidence of coordination with rehabilitation staff.
  • Must be able to communicate clearly and accurately both orally and in writing.

Requirements/Qualifications:

  • Bachelors degree in related health field, thirty (30) hours or their equivalent of college credit
    towards a bachelor’s degree in a health related field, or one year of work experience
    in a supervised mental health setting.
  • Must yield a positive criminal background check and have a Maryland driver’s license
    with less than 3 points.
  • Must be willing to go through a fingerprinting background check.
  • Must have own reliable transportation to assist clients with medical appointments – as needed.
  • Selected candidates must complete sixty (60) hours of paid on-the-job training prior to working
    with clients independently and participate in monthly supervisions and staff meetings.
